My First Day at The Iron Yard

I feel my first day went pretty well. I still need to get comfortable with Git, but I don't feel lost in HTML or CSS. I attribute that to the prework being fantastic. I was able to complete the first 2 levels rather quickly. I only made it through about half of level 3. For the first 2 levels, I was able to do it while on the job working security (not much happens on 3rd shift in Nashville). Trying to complete prework from home with a 2 year old and a 2 month old is... challenging! I much preferred the videos in Code School, but Code Academy provided a good base. I was looking forward to Treehouse, but simply ran out of time. I will try to go back and take their courses too. You can never learn too much.
